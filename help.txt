```
Usage: /mathconf <help|nopsst|revoke|authorize>

  help:
    Displays this help information.
    
  nopsst:
    No longer displays a nudge when you post on an un-authenticated account.
    
  revoke:
    Revokes and removes the authentication we have for your account.
    
  authorize:
    Provides a link with which you can authorize our app to post on your behalf. NOTE: this is not required to use the app, but it is reccomended. 
```
